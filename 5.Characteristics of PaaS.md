**Platform as a Service (PaaS)** is a cloud computing model that provides a platform for developers to **build, test, deploy, and manage applications** without worrying about the underlying infrastructure.

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è Key Characteristics of PaaS

### 1. **Multi-User Accessibility**

Multiple users can access and work on the same development environment simultaneously, enabling efficient collaboration among teams.

### 2. **Integration with Web Services and Databases**

PaaS platforms easily integrate with various **web services, APIs, and databases**, simplifying app development and data management.

### 3. **Scalability through Virtualization**

Built on **virtualization technology**, PaaS allows resources to be scaled up or down automatically as per workload or business needs.

### 4. **Support for Multiple Languages and Frameworks**

PaaS supports various **programming languages and frameworks** such as Java, Python, PHP, and .NET, making it flexible for developers.

### 5. **Auto-Scaling and Resource Management**

PaaS can automatically allocate or release computing resources depending on application demand, ensuring high performance.
